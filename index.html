About Me

I am Kyle Wicker, an artist who creates digital art for t-shirts, websites, and other digital applications. I am also a mural artist, specializing in realism, abstract, and aboriginal art. I am constantly testing my boundaries. In addition, I manage a traditional aboriginal dance troupe that performs welcomes to country and cultural days at schools, sharing and teaching culture, culminating in a one-hour performance for the entire school.
